[F18896]
Mr. and Mrs. Bertram Littlefield, of 379 Blauvelt Road, Pearl River, celebrated their twenty-fifth wedding anniversary Sunday afternoon with an open house at their home attended by 65 relatives, friends and neighbors. In keeping with the 25-year festivities, silver decorations were displayed throughout the home and a large silver and white cake was presented to the couple.
Guests of honor at the occasion were Mr. Littlefield's parents. Mr. and Mrs. Walter Littlefield, of Haverhill, Mass., who celebrated their golden wedding anniversary two years ago. Another Special guest was Mrs. Littlefield's sister, Mrs. Florence Haskell, of Manchester, New Hampshire.
Originally, both Littlefields resided in Massachusetts They have lived in Pearl River for over twelve years, coming here from St. Albans, Long Island. The present Littlefield home was purchased in 1949. They have two children, David H., who is presently attending Western Maryland College, Westminster, MD, and Cynthia, 16, who is a student at Pearl River High School.
Orangetown Telegram, 10 December1959
