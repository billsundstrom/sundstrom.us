My dear Cousin -

The little verse on the Christmas card expresses my wish for you and yours. I hope you are able to have all your family with you on Christmas day and will have a happy time. My family is small - one son, his wife, and four grandchildren all grown up but like children on Christmas day - ready for a good time and happy to be together.

The oldest daughter is married and lives in Princeton, N.J. She cannot be with us on the 25th as she has a baby son who is too small for the trip in our New England winter - It will be the first break in our family gatherings - My husband and I spend our time trying to keep warm and succeed fairly well but like summer time better. We are very comfortable for people of our age.

Merry Christmas to all,

Cousin Esther Witherell
