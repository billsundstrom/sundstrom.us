Lucy's Letters

Nancy Partridge Miller


Charles Franklin Miller and his Sister - Harriet

These 58 letters were written between 1870 and 1935. Four letters were written by Nancy Partridge Miller to her son, Charles Franklin Miller. Most of the remaining letters are from Louisa or Charles Miller to their daughter, Lucy Clewett. There are a few other authors: Lucy’s sister, Celia Chamberlain, and several cousins -- Alice Craven, Ned Hoxie, George Hoxie, and Ester Witherell.
An account of the discovery of the letters and their preservation project is available here.
