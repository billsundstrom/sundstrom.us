Hubert LD Smith, Jr., age 53, of Rhinelander, died on Jan. 29, 2014, at Taylor Park.
Hubert was born Jan. 5, 1961, in Rhinelander, to Hubert LD and Delores (Durkee) Smith Sr.

He owned and operated "Hugh's Designs" until his declining health prevented him from doing so. Hubert's ill health did not prevent him from showing his love for his Savior. He did so as a longtime member of the Church of Christ. He was a member of the church since 1989.

Hubert loved and cherished all of these children as if they were his own.
Hubert was preceded in death by his parents and his sister-in-law, Susan (O'Melia) Jankowski.
A memorial service for Hubert will be held at 11 a.m., with a visitation from 10 to 11 a.m., on Saturday, Feb. 1, 2014, all at the Church of Christ at 1103 Thayer Street in Rhinelander.
Pastor Jeremy Monk will officiate. You may leave your private condolences for the Smith family at www.carlsonfh.com. The Carlson Funeral Home (715-369-1414) is assisting the Smith family with the arrangements.

John Frank SMITH

[I116669]
John Frank Smith, son of Owen Otto and Reva Bernadine (Louthan) Smith, was born December 27, 1949 at Watonga, OK. He died of a sudden illness on Monday, August 1, 2011 at the Seiling Community Hospital. He was 61 years of age.
John was raised at Seiling where he attended Seiling School graduating with the class of 1968. He attended Okmulgee Vo-Tech School where he completed courses in auto body. He entered the United States Army and was stationed in Germany. He received his honorable discharge and returned to Oklahoma. He furthered his education with a degree in political science from the University of Central Oklahoma in Edmond. While attending college he worked for Little Giant Pump Company ten years. He returned to Seiling where he was employed by Gene Ward and later he worked for Phillips Petroleum. He owned and operated the Kerr-McGee service station in Seiling before going to work in 1994 for Dewey County District 1 as a heavy equipment operator. Some of John's hobbies were gardening, he was an avid reader and he enjoyed music of all types. He was well known for his book and music collections.
He was united in marriage to Cynthia Ann "Cindy" Hedges July 31, 1981 at the First Christian Church in Seiling. They made their home near Seiling where they raised their three children: Jessi, Emily and Ben.
He is survived by his wife Cindy Smith, two daughters, Jessi Bigler and husband Michael of Warrensburg, MO and Emily Harrell and husband Zachary of Cashion, one son, Ben Smith of Seiling; his mother, Bernadine Smith of Seiling, three grandchildren, Elijah Joe and Finley Jackson Harrell and Chloe Ann Bigler, one sister, Inez Sander and husband Lindy of Seiling and one brother, Ray Smith and wife Ruth of Taloga. He is also survived by many other relatives and friends. He was preceded in death by his father, "Deacon" Smith June 17, 1997.
The funeral service was held at 10:00 a.m. Friday, August 5, 2011 at the Elm Grove Community Church with Pastors Orville White and Dennis Adair, officiating. Interment with military honors followed in the Brumfield Cemetery at Seiling.
