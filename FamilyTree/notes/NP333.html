Annalee Etta LUCAS

[I017273]
WILLMAR -- Annalee Schleusner, 71, of Willmar, formerly of Hector, died Monday at Birchwood in Hutchinson.
Funeral Service will be 11 a.m. Friday at Redeemer Lutheran Church in Willmar.
Visitation will be from 5 to 8 p.m. today at Hughes Funeral Home in Hector and for one hour prior to the service at the church.
Annalee E. Schleusner was born Nov. 27, 1938, in Hector Township, Renville County, the daughter of Clarence and Lillie (Henke) Lucas. She was baptized and confirmed at Osceola Church of Hope. Annalee grew up in the Hector area, and started District 90 School in Osceola Township in 1944. She transferred to the Hector High School after it was built in 1952, and graduated with the class of 1956.
On Nov. 8, 1958, Annalee was united in marriage to David Schleusner at Zion Lutheran Church in Olivia. The couple lived numerous places throughout the United States. Annalee worked as a rural mail carrier in the Hector, Buffalo Lake and Prior Lake areas. For the last five years, Annalee and David lived in Willmar. On July 7, 2010, Annalee moved into Birchwood Hospice House in Hutchinson.
Annalee was an active member of First Lutheran Church in Hector and served on the church council. She was a member of the Redeemer Lutheran Church in Willmar at the time of her death. She was a member of the Rural Letter Carriers Association and also of the Hector Horsemen Club. She enjoyed gardening, going to Canterbury, and she especially enjoyed watching her grandchildren race. She loved her family and grandchildren.
Annalee is survived by her husband, David Schleusner of Willmar; son, Douglas Schleusner and his wife, Gail, of Hector; grandchildren, Miranda Schleusner of St. Cloud and Ryan Schleusner of Hector; brothers, LaVerne (Cindy) Lucas of Courtland and Ira (Sandy) Lucas of Medord.
Annalee was preceded in death by her parents.
West Central Tribune, September 2010
