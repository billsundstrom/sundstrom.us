Katherine HOSMER

[I060848]
Katherine married John S. Stoddard in 1849 in New York. Their first child Edward was born in January of 1850 in Brooklyn, Kings, NY. Then their daughter Sarah Elizabeth was born there in 1852, then their son Elias in 1853. Elias died in about 1855. Katherine and John are listed on the 1855 NY state census and the 1860 federal census living in Brooklyn. John died in 1862 and Katherine and children, Edward and Sarah, are listed on the NY state census still in Brooklyn in 1865. Katherine then remarried Willis B. Edson in Illinois in 1867. They then moved to Pulaski County, Illinois with her son Edward and daughter Sarah and are listed on the 1870 and 1880 census there. Son Edward died in Pulaski County and was buried at Villa Ridge in 1888. Daughter Sarah married and moved to New York and eventually Katherine went to live with her and son-in-law Charles Austin Fosdick in Chautaugua County, NY. She is listed on the 1900 census there living with Sarah and Charles. Sarah died in 1904 and is buried in Westfield Cemetery in Westfield, Chautaugua County. Katherine went back to Villa Ridge to visit with friends and family in 1905 and died there in the home of her granddaughter Mrs. George P. Hartwell, daughter of Edward.
