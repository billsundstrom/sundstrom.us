Dr. Elaine Alexandra Athos, a daughter of Gloria F. Athos and Dr. Laurence Athos of Pleasanton, Calif., was married Saturday to Dylan Stimpson Brown, a son of Leslie Ahern Brown and Wendell S. Brown III of Marion, Mass. The Rev. Kenneth J. Suibielski, a Roman Catholic priest, performed the ceremony at the St. Clare Church in Westerly, R.I.
The couple met at Brown University, from which they both graduated.
The bride, 28, who is known as Alexandra, will take her husband's name. Next month, she is to begin an internship at St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center in New York. She received a medical degree from George Washington University.
The bride's father is a pulmonologist in private practice in Castro Valley, Calif.
The bridegroom, 29, is to join the New York law firm Cahill Gordon & Reindel as an associate in January. He received a law degree magna cum laude from George Mason University.
The bridegroom's father is a professor of ocean sciences at the University of Massachusetts, Dartmouth.
The New York Times, 23 May 2010

SHINGLEHOUSE - Miss Doris Marie Murray, daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Fred Murray, and Gordon Hawkes, son of Mr. and Mrs. Lorne Hawkes, were united in marriage December 18, 1948.
The ceremony was performed at the bride's home by the Rev. George Sickenberger.
The bride wore an aqua suit with a corsage of pink roses and white mums. Mrs. Leslie Ellsworth, maid of honor for her sister, chose a fuchsia suit with a corsage of red and white carnations. Leslie Ellsworth acted as best man.
Following the ceremony a reception and shower were held and the couple received many gifts. The couple will reside in Westons Mills. Guests were present from Alma, NY, Shinglehouse, PA, Bradford, PA, Coleville, PA, Eldred, PA, and Olean, NY
