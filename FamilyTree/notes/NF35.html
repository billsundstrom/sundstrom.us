[F12741]
Stockton--A beautiful summer wedding took place Saturday afternoon at 2 o'clock at the Stockton Federated church, when Miss Lillian Christiansen, daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Jens Christiansen of Stockton became the bride of Russell Carpenter of Sinclairville, son of Mrs. Nellie Carpenter.
Rev. C. K. Shoup performed the double ring ceremony in the presence of about 300 guests.
Evergreens with ferns and baskets of pink and white peonies, with lighted white candles banked the altar.
The maid of honor was Mrs. Robert Carpenter and the bridesmaids were Elverts Turk and Phylis Wieler flower girl was Linda Lee Putnam. The organist was Mr. Moyer. Maynard Waite of Jamestown sang, "I Love You Truly" and "Oh Promise Me."
The bride wore a white jersey gown with a train. Robert Carpenter was best man Milton Putnam and Burton Carpenter were ushers.
A reception was given in the casement of the church to about one hundred and eighty guests. Mr. and Mrs. Carpenter will reside in Sinclairville.
The Fredonia Censor, 26 June 1947
