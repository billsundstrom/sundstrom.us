Mr. and Mrs. Raymond Bern of New Rochelle, N.Y., celebrated their 50th wedding anniversary with a gala dinner party attended by 135 guests at the Greentree Country Club. Friends and family from eleven states and Vancouver, British Columbia, joined in the festivities.
Mrs. Bern, the former Betsy Sullivan of Bridgeport, Conn., and Mr. Bern were married on June 24, 1954, at Sacred Heart Church in Bridgeport. They first met on a bus when she was in high school and he was a student at the University of Bridgeport from which he graduated. Five years later they were married. After a wedding trip to Bermuda they lived in New Rochelle where they still reside.
Raymond, after serving in the Army Air Corps before college, spent many years in the direct mail business and now still works in the credit-collection field. A lifelong resident of New Rochelle, he was for many years senior tennis champ of New Rochelle.
Betsy graduated from New Haven State Teachers College, now Southern Connecticut State University, and has taken education courses at New York University. She taught at Mt. Tom Day School and was the director of the Scarsdale Congregational Church Nursery School for many years. She retired from Blessed Sacrament-St. Gabriel High School after teaching there for 20 years. She is now a volunteer at the Sound Shore Medical Center and a Literacy Volunteer of Westchester at the New Rochelle Library.
They are the proud parents of Cathryn F. Bern, C.S.W., of Albany, N.Y., and Dr. Paul H. Bern of Syracuse.
NYJ News

Kimberly S. Parr and Dr. Paul H. Bern were married on June 9, 2007, at Erwin First United Methodist Church in Syracuse, N.Y. Officiating was the Reverend Wendy Rhodehamel.
The bride is the daughter of Jerry and Carolyn Parr of Washington, D.C. The groom is the son of Raymond and Betsy Bern of New Rochelle, N.Y.
Honor attendants were Jennifer Parr Turek and Trish Parr Smith, sisters of the bride. Ellie Marie Smith was the flower girl. Ushers were David Balfoort and Chris Slawecki, friends of the groom. The groom's sister and brother-in-law, Cathryn Bern-Smith and Steven V. Smith of Glenville, N.Y., were soloists at the ceremony.
The bride is a graduate of Syracuse University and received an M.B.A. from LeMoyne College. She is the creative group supervisor at Latorra, Paul and McCann Advertising. The groom is a graduate of Iona Prep School. He received his B.A. from Villanova University, his M.A. from Fordham University, and his Ph.D. from Syracuse University. He is the numeric data services librarian at Syracuse University.
The couple resides in Syracuse.
